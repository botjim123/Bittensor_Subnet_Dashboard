Index Methodology
Index Family
8 indexes — 7 sector indexes + Total Market (BSACS-TM)
Base Date
September 4, 2025 — all indexes start at 1000.00
Formula
Index(t) = Index(t−1) × Σ( weight(i) × [price(i,t) / price(i,t−1)] ) for all eligible constituents
Weighting
Market cap-weighted daily. Weight = prior-day USD market cap of subnet / total sector market cap. Weights sum to 1.0 each day.
Rebalancing
Continuous daily. No fixed schedule.
New Entrant Rule
Weight = 0 on first day of appearance. Enters fully from day 2.
Eligibility
Subnet must have a verified public description. Subnets without documentation flagged Unclassified and excluded.
Exclusions
SN0 (Root network) excluded — not an investable subnet.
Price Data
USD price from Taostats Pool API. AMM price from on-chain liquidity pool × TAO/USD rate from CoinGecko.
Classification
Primary subnet function. 3-tier taxonomy: Sector → Industry Group → Industry. Based on verified public description.
